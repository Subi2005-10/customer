Frequently Asked Questions

What data format is required for CSV upload?

Your CSV file should include columns like gender, SeniorCitizen, Partner, Dependents, tenure, PhoneService, InternetService, Contract, PaymentMethod, MonthlyCharges, and TotalCharges. See the Upload CSV page for a complete list.

How accurate is the churn prediction model?

Our KNN model is trained on telecom customer data and typically achieves high accuracy. The model considers multiple factors to provide reliable predictions with confidence scores.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes, all predictions are stored locally in a SQLite database. Your data is processed securely and is not shared with third parties.

Can I download prediction results?

Yes, when you upload a CSV file, the results are automatically saved and can be downloaded from the uploads folder.
