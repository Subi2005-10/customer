About Churn Prediction Website

What is Customer Churn?

Customer churn, also known as customer attrition, refers to when customers stop doing business with a company. In the telecommunications industry, churn is a critical metric that directly impacts revenue and growth.

Identifying customers who are likely to churn allows businesses to implement targeted retention strategies, improve customer satisfaction, and reduce revenue loss.

Our Machine Learning Model

Churn Prediction Website uses a K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) classifier, a powerful machine learning algorithm that identifies patterns in customer data to predict churn likelihood.

The model is trained on telecom customer data and considers various factors including:

  • Customer demographics (gender, age, partner status)
  • Service usage (tenure, phone service, internet service)
  • Contract details (contract type, payment method)
  • Financial metrics (monthly charges, total charges)
  • Additional services (streaming, security, backup)
